Post-Recovery Steps

After recovering your account, follow these steps to secure it:

  1. Change Your Password: Create a strong, unique password that you haven't used before. Use a password manager to generate and store it securely.
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Go to Settings > Security and Login > Two-Factor Authentication. Choose either an authenticator app or SMS for the second factor.
  3. Review Active Sessions: Check Settings > Security and Login > Where You're Logged In. Log out of any suspicious sessions.
  4. Update Contact Information: Add and verify multiple email addresses and phone numbers in Settings > General > Contact.
  5. Review Privacy Settings: Check Settings > Privacy to ensure your account's visibility and sharing settings are appropriate.
  6. Check Account Activity: Review your recent activity for any unauthorized changes or posts.
  7. Set Up Login Alerts: Enable notifications for unrecognized logins in Settings > Security and Login > Get alerts about unrecognized logins.
  8. Review Trusted Contacts: Set up trusted contacts who can help you recover your account if needed.
