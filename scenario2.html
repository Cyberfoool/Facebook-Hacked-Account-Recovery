Scenario 2: You have contact info but need a code

If your email/phone is correct but you're asked for a code you can't access, do this:

  1. Reset your password by clicking scenario 1 instructions.
  2. After entering your password, When asked for a code, click "Try another way".
  3. Now add a new contact method. Use a "new" email or phone to get your link to unlock your account.
  4. You can choose following Govt and non-Govt ID for ID verification.
  5. After verification, you can will be sent a link to your email/phone to unlock your account.
  6. Finally after you unlock your account, turn on two-factor authentication on your device.
